Elastomeric Roofing Coatings

Elastomeric roof coatings are sprayed on to provide a long-term, durable weatherproofing and maintenance, and protection from UV rays. The material acts as a waterproof, flexible coating that also seals out air and moisture. Elastomeric coatings can also be used in industrial applications such as tank lining, above/below grade walls, safety barriers and roofing systems.

Concrete Staining & Painting

Concrete Staining & Painting services offer a solution to fix your concrete issues. We can help you restore and rejuvenate your concrete to its original beauty. Our process begins with a uniform acid etch, where the existing concrete is stripped down to provide an even surface for your new finish. Next, we apply our unique formulation of high quality pigments, resins and hardeners that will help protect and enhance your concrete's color and texture.
